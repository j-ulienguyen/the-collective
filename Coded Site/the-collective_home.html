Our Story

At The Collective, we offer a very unique experience by not only serving up your classic cup of coffee, but alcohol as well. If a regular cup of joe isn’t going to cut it, indulge in one of our specialty coffee cocktails made with a shot (or two!) of Baileys.


With a strong passion for coffee (and alcohol!), we are constantly striving to provide you with the best coffee experience tailored to your tastes. We are proud to serve our special house-blend of coffee beans which have been sourced from around the world and roasted to perfection in-house.


With the focus of building and inspiring a community filled with passionate individuals, we want The Collective to be a place you can proudly call your second home.
